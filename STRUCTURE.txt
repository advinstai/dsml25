Purpose:
This repository serves as the central hub for all materials related to the
4th School on Data Science and Machine Learning at ICTP-SAIFR, November 16-21, 2025.

Usage:
1. Lecturers add materials to lectures/dayX-topic/
2. Organizers maintain exercises/topic/ directories
3. Participants clone, install dependencies, and work through materials
4. All stakeholders can contribute via pull requests
