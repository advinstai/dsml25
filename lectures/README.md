# Lectures

This directory contains all lecture materials for the 4th School on Data Science and Machine Learning.

## Directory Structure

- **day0-toolbox/**: Sunday - Toolbox Kickoff
  - Pandas - Data Wrangling 101
  - Matplotlib - Data Visualization
  - PyTorch - Tensors & Computation

- **day1-foundations/**: Monday - Foundations of Neural Networks
  - Statistical Methods for Data Analysis I & II
  - Neural Networks 101
  - The Perceptron

- **day2-deeper/**: Tuesday - Going Deeper with Neural Networks
  - From Single to Multi-Layer Networks
  - Backpropagation Explained
  - Activation functions and network training
  - Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) I

- **day3-architectures/**: Wednesday - Specialized Architectures
  - Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) II
  - Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs)
  - Transformers
  - Large Language Models and generative AI

- **day4-applications/**: Thursday - Applications and Domain-Specific Uses
  - AI in natural sciences
  - AI for the Climate Emergency
  - AI in humanities and social sciences
  - The Role of HPC in AI

- **day5-frontiers/**: Friday - Frontiers and Future Directions
  - Foundation Models
  - Physics-enhanced Machine Learning
  - Explainable AI
  - Ethics in AI Panel

## File Format

Lecture materials may include:
- PDF slides
- Jupyter notebooks
- Supplementary reading materials
- Links to external resources

## Contributing

Lecturers should place their materials in the appropriate day folder with descriptive filenames following the pattern:
`session-topic-lecturer.ext`

Example: `morning-statistical-methods-i.pdf`
