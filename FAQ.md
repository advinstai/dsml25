# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Application and Registration

### Q: Is there a registration fee?
**A**: No, there is no registration fee for this school.

### Q: When is the application deadline?
**A**: The application deadline is September 16, 2025.

### Q: How do I apply?
**A**: Apply online at: https://sis.ictp-saifr.org/applications/dsml2025

### Q: Will financial support be provided?
**A**: Limited funds are available for travel and local expenses. Indicate your need when applying.

### Q: What is the selection criteria?
**A**: Selection is based on academic merit, motivation, and relevance of the school to your research.

## Prerequisites and Preparation

### Q: Do I need prior machine learning experience?
**A**: No, but basic Python programming and statistics knowledge are recommended.

### Q: What should I install before the school?
**A**: Python 3.8+, Jupyter, and the libraries listed in `requirements.txt`. See PARTICIPANT_INFO.md for details.

### Q: What programming language will be used?
**A**: Python, with PyTorch as the primary deep learning framework.

### Q: Will the school be in English?
**A**: Yes, all sessions will be conducted in English.

## Poster Presentation

### Q: Is the poster presentation mandatory?
**A**: Yes, all participants must present a research poster.

### Q: What size should my poster be?
**A**: Maximum 1m (width) x 1.5m (length), printed as a banner. A4/A3 paper is NOT accepted.

### Q: What if my research doesn't use machine learning yet?
**A**: That's fine! Present your current research and explain where ML might be applicable.

### Q: When will I present my poster?
**A**: Posters will be displayed throughout the school and discussed during coffee breaks and lunch.
